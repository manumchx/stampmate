Got questions?

Do my customers need to download an app?

No. Customers scan your QR code and the card is added to Apple or Google Wallet in one tap. No downloads, no accounts, no friction.

How do I add stamps when a customer visits?

From your dashboard, find the customer and tap plus 1 stamp. Takes 3 seconds. Faster than paper.

What if I already have paper stamp cards?

Put our QR code on your counter. Most customers switch immediately once they see how easy it is.

Is there a contract or lock-in?

No lock-in, no contracts. Pay month to month and cancel anytime. We are confident you will stay because it works.
